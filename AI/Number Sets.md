
These ideas were suggested by Georg Kantor

Natural Numbers
- the number of natural numbers: alef0
- the number of prime number is equal to the number of natural numbers

Integers
- the number of natural numbers: alef0 (because of a bijection)

Rational Numbers
- the number of rational number is still alef0 (because of a bijection)

Real Numbers
- the number of real numbers between 0 and 1 is still alef0

Complex Numbers
- the number of complex numbers is the same as the number of real numbers
